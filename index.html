All attacks hit for -1 damage.Social Proof. When you kill an enemy, lower the Motivation of other enemies in the group by d2.You are selling:
The scent of non-existing flowers.
Once a day, you can choose to use the one of the following tactics:
  1. Foot in the Door. Both during and outside of battle, you can ask for something small (napkin, penny etc.) followed by a bigger request (for one to kill themselves, attack a friend, surrender, etc.). The first ask will be automatically successful. In the following round, test Soft Skills DR16 for the second ask.
  2. Long Pitch. Start pitching. Doesn't matter what you say, just try to get people to listen. All enemies skip their action.
  3. Discount. Loot an additional d66x10¤ from the fallen enemy.
  4. Door-in-the-face. Ask someone for something big. Test their Motivation and Soft Skills DR12. If both tests are successful, the target will do what they were asked to do.
  5. Understanding Pain Points. Analyze an enemy. Skip one turn. Next turn, you deal +d6 damage to the analyzed opponent.
  6. FOMO (Fear of Misplaced Omens). Harness the fear of your enemies by offering them a limited deal (e.g., run now or be brutally violated later). Test their Motivation this turn.
